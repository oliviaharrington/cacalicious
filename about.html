About me!

Hi I'm Patricia O'Flaherty from "A Touch of Magic" based in Carrigaline, County Cork.

I consider myself a sugar artist and I create bespoke wedding and special occasion cakes with a contemporary elegant twist, from couture cookies to couture cupcakes, sculpted chocolate, cocoa butter painting and edible works of art a speciality!

Committed to using only the finest of ingredients and freshly baked to order. Gold medalist for both my wedding and novelty cakes I am also now teaching my craft.

Be assured that you needn't miss out if you have special dietary requirements, I also offer gluten free cakes using the range of flavours and fillings available from A Touch of Magic.

My cakes are made to make your mouth order!
